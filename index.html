Our Story

Bridging the Gap Between Talent and Opportunity.

AKTDC was established in 2023 with a simple yet powerful belief: talent is distributed equally, but opportunity is not. We saw bright young minds in rural villages struggling not because they lacked intelligence, but because they lacked guidance and resources.

We started with a vision to democratize education. By bringing high-quality coaching and mentorship to areas with limited resources, we are helping students from the heart of India achieve their academic dreams.
